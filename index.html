Responsive Redesign:

Zara Women’s Shoes “View All” Webpage

A redesign of a Zara webpage with considerations to user preference and responsiveness of the webpage.

USABILITY OF THE WEB PAGE

Zara’s Women’s Shoes page is annoying because the images are huge, making you scroll forever, and not all the shoes are visible—you have to search just to find them.

ACCESSIBILITY

Evaluating the accessibility of Zara's "Women's Shoes - View All" webpage using WAVE (Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool) guidelines reveals several areas of concern:

Alternative Text for Images: Many product images lack descriptive alternative text, making it difficult for screen readers to convey visual content to users with visual impairments.

Form Labels: Some interactive elements, such as filters and buttons, are missing proper labels, leading to confusion for users relying on assistive technologies.
